Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Eagle Ninja?

    Eagle Ninja is a platform for collaborating with Quest Sponsors (brands and retailers) by telling them what works and what does not, what you like and what you do not like, and in general helping improve the products and services you are most interested in.

    You do this by being invited to Quests that are nearby and of interest to you, and by accepting to participate in those you like in return for cash or Shuriken.

  • What is a Quest?

    A quest is an invitation to help improve a product, service or advertisement by going to a nearby location, checking out a product or service, viewing an image or video, taking a picture or a scan, and/or answering a few brief questions.

    There are two types of Quests, Cash Quests, and Shuriken Quests.

  • When do I get Invited to Cash Quests?

    The more complete your profile, the higher the chances of being invited to a Cash Quest.

    You will be invited when you are nearby the location where a Cash Quest needs to be performed, and if that moment is not a good time for you, you can reserve a slot so that you can do the Quest later at your convenience.

  • What is a Shuriken Quest?

    You earn Shuriken for certain types of Quests, as well as when you complete fields in your profile. The higher the accumulated Shuriken, the higher your ranking within the Eagle Ninja community.

    At certain times you may be able to exchange your Shuriken for valuable discounts, promotions and loyalty points such as air-miles.

  • How can I increase the number and value of Quests I am invited to?

    The more complete your profile, the higher the probability of being invited to a Quest, and to the most valuable Quests.

  • What is a Follower?

    A follower is someone who joined Eagle Ninja as a result of your invitation. You will receive a bonus cash payment of 10% of the earnings of all your followers, for as long as they are earning cash through Eagle Ninja; How cool is that!?

  • How do I get paid?

    You will need to have a paypal account and provide permission for us to send payments to you through PayPal.

  • How do I redeem Shuriken?

    Shuriken are redeemable on specific occasions when you are offered the ability to exchange them for coupons, discounts and loyalty points including air-miles.

  • How long are Quests available for?

    It varies, some may be available for a few days, even a week, and some for only a few hours.

  • What triggers a Quest?

    We focus a lot of effort on only inviting you to Quests that we believe will be of interest, and that are easy and fun to do, so we use your profile information, date & time of day, and location.

    Although we do NOT store any data concerning your browsing history, the URLs you visit may change a unique numeric "Matching Score" associated with your profile which will also affect the Quests you are invited to.

  • Will Quests cost me money?

    No. You do not need to pay us, the brand, or the retailer any money in order to participate in a Quest.
